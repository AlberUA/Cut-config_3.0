How to Get Started

To get started with CUT Configurator, the first step is to register on the CUT Configurator website. Registration is simple and will only take a few minutes. After registration, upload data about your project to CUT Configurator, including drawings, material specifications, and any other necessary information.

Once your data is uploaded, you can adjust the parameters according to your cutting requirements. Specify details such as material type, stock sizes, cutting angle requirements, and more.

  • With your parameters set, you can then proceed to create a cutting plan by clicking the designated button. CUT Configurator will automatically generate an optimal cutting plan based on the parameters you've provided.
  • After the cutting plan is generated, take the time to review it thoroughly. Evaluate its effectiveness and assess whether any adjustments are necessary for further optimization.
  • Throughout the process, you'll find that CUT Configurator offers an intuitive interface, making the creation of cutting plans quick and straightforward. With its advanced capabilities, such as data import/export and report generation, managing the cutting process becomes even more streamlined.

In conclusion, by following these steps and utilizing the features offered by CUT Configurator, you'll be able to maximize resource utilization and enhance efficiency in your material cutting processes.
