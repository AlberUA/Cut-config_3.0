Cut-Configurator was created for...

Cut-Configurator is designed for a wide range of businesses and industries where cutting materials plays an important role. It is useful for furniture manufacturers, metalworking, textile industries and other fields. In particular, it is a valuable tool for production managers, engineers and machine operators who are interested in process optimization and cost reduction.

Cut-Configurator was created to optimize material cutting processes, and here's why. It helps you make the most of each material, reduces cutting time and minimizes waste. This means saving resources and reducing costs. Thanks to flexible settings and an intuitive interface, CUT Configurator becomes an indispensable tool for increasing production efficiency.
